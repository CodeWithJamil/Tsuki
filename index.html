About Us

What is Tsuki。?

Tsuki。is a business brand that focuses on cakes, we provide more than 200+ different types of cakes from all over the world, from sweet to very sweet.

Tsuki。has been around since 2023, on September 6 to be precise. We opened our first cake shop on Jalan A. P. Pettarani, Makassar, South Sulawesi, Indonesia. With effort and hard work, we are committed to spreading happiness to everyone through the unrivaled taste of our cake shop
